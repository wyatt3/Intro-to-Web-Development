About Us

The Logan Library is a community hub that provides access to a wide range of resources and services to support lifelong learning, creativity, and connection. Our mission is to empower individuals and enrich our community by providing access to information, fostering creativity, and promoting lifelong learning. We offer a variety of programs and services for all ages, including book clubs, storytime for kids, technology workshops, and more. Our library is also a great place to study, work on projects, or simply relax with a good book. We are committed to creating an inclusive and welcoming environment for everyone in our community.
